# Evaluate

Deploying an AI agent is just the first step - Evaluate empowers businesses to analyze, refine, and optimize AI-driven customer interactions over time. By leveraging AI-powered insights, businesses can ensure their AI agents continuously improve, delivering higher resolution rates and better customer satisfaction.

In this section, you can explore:

* [**Insights**](insights.md): Identify top customer inquiries, track AI agent resolution rates, and measure CSAT impact to uncover key conversation trends.
* [**Conversations**](conversations/): Review individual AI-led interactions to assess effectiveness, detect failures, and validate resolution outcomes.
* [**Flagged messages**](flagged-messages.md): Keep track of messages that could potentially impact a safe and positive customer experience.
