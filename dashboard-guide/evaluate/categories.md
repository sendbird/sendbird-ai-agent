# Categories

This feature provides insights into customer interactions with AI Agents, helping businesses understand conversation trends, resolution rates, and customer satisfaction. By analyzing categorized interactions, businesses can identify common issues, track AI performance, and make data-driven improvements.

**Note:** Categories are pre-defined by the business during onboarding and can be configured. Once conversations begin, they are automatically categorized by AI Agents according to your category definition.

## Analyze conversations

For optimal analysis, leverage the following data points.

* Category level: See conversation categories, such as Plan Upgrade, Cancel Membership, or Account Hold Activation. By clicking on each row of the table, you can delve into its subcategories to probe the data
* Metrics by category: Identify top-performing and underperforming sub categories based on resolution rates, customer satisfaction (CSAT), resolution times, and handoff rates to human agents.
* Conversation level: Dive into individual conversations to understand how AI agents are responding and performing for top-performing and underperforming categories.

## **Optimize AI Agents**

* Improve AI Agents by adding or updating knowledge, actionbooks, tools or safeguards.
* Adjust AI handling for specific categories in the [Knowledge](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1GNQ6eMmjyWLV2A9xnTgtobbFb5tYgtm_ASvDUU_hw1g/edit?tab=t.0#heading=h.hn9g5il25rxm) or [Actionbooks](../build/actionbooks.md) page to improve efficiency and reduce human escalation.
