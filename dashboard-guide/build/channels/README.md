# Channels

In **Channels**, you can see a collection of channels in which AI Agents can carry out end-to-end conversations and handle user queries. Sendbird AI agent currently supports Messenger, SMS, Email, and WhatsApp. Channels such as Voice and Kakao Talk will be available soon.

***

## Channel types

Sendbird AI agent can assist users in real-time through the following channels:

* [**Messenger**](messenger/): Implement an in-app channel for AI agent's live chat on your client app using Sendbird Chat SDK or UIKit.
* [**Voice**](voice.md): Enable your AI agent to engage in real-time phone conversations and assist users through voice calls.
* [**SMS**](sms.md): Add a phone number to allow your AI agent to assist users through text messages.
* [**Email**](email.md): Save your company's email domain and custom sender email address, enabling your AI agent to send and receive inquiry emails from users on behalf of you.
* [**WhatsApp**](whatsapp.md): Connect your WhatsApp Business Account to Sendbird AI agent for streamlined customer interactions and improved communication efficiency.
